HOW TO USE THIS TOOL

1. Search — Enter a city and pick an industry (or type any keyword). Hit Search to find local businesses.

2. Review — Results show name, rating, address, phone, and whether they have a website. No website = your best lead.

3. Save — Click "Save to Pipeline" on any business you want to target.

4. Email — Click "Generate Email" to get a ready-to-send cold pitch tailored to their industry.

5. Track — Switch to the Pipeline tab to see your leads by status and update as you make contact.

6. Export — Download your full prospect list as CSV anytime.

First time? Make sure Maps JavaScript API is enabled in your Google Cloud Console alongside the Places API. Your API key is already saved in this file.
