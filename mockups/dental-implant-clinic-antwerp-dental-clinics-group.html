PRECISION
Cone Beam 3D imaging

Our Cone Beam produces 3D "cuts" of your maxillaries — guiding implants to within 1/10 mm.

Several SIRONA (ex-SIEMENS) Cone Beam scanners let us see your jaw in three dimensions, then plan and guide each implant with software precision. It is the difference between placing an implant — and placing it exactly where it belongs.

3D Cone Beam cuts

Volumetric imaging of your maxillaries for accurate diagnosis and planning.

Guided implants

Software-guided rehabilitation accurate to within 1/10 mm.

Minimal radiation

Digital, AFCN/FANC-approved X-rays — low dose, instant, perfect images.

Digital dentistry

SIRONA intra-oral scanners & zirconium crowns — perfect fit, no more paste impressions.

For fixed prosthetics we take digital impressions with intra-oral scanners, guaranteeing the perfect seat of your zirconium crowns and bridges. Our substantial turnover means we buy — and give you access to — the best German and American equipment.
